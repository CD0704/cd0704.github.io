论文摘要 (中文)

弗朗茨·卡夫卡是20世纪文坛上的杰出人物，以其独特的叙事技巧闻名，巧妙地将个人经历与超现实主义维度交织在一起。他的代表作品《审判》正是这种独特风格的典范。这项研究旨在探索《审判》中家庭权威主义的影响，展现卡夫卡与父亲之间紧张关系对叙事结构产生的深远影响。

本文旨在深入挖掘卡夫卡个人经历在叙事中的痕迹，精心剖析反映他自身家庭困境的错综复杂的父子关系。通过采用这种解读视角，我们的努力旨在提供对卡夫卡文学才华的细腻理解，同时揭示他作品中潜藏的心理基础。

Abstract (English)

Franz Kafka, an eminent luminary of 20th-century literary circles, is celebrated for his distinctive narrative prowess that intricately weaves personal chronicles with surrealistic dimensions. His magnum opus, "The Judgment," epitomizes this distinct stylistic imprint. This study embarks on an exploration into the reverberations of familial authoritarianism within "The Judgment," unfurling the profound impact of Kafka's fraught relationship with his patriarch on the narrative tapestry.

This paper aims to plumb the depths of Kafka's personal odyssey imprinted within the narrative, meticulously parsing the intricate father-son dynamics that mirror the echoes of his own familial tribulations. By adopting this interpretative lens, our endeavor aspires to proffer a nuanced comprehension of Kafka's literary brilliance while unraveling the psychological substratum underpinning his oeuvre.
